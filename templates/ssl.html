SSL Initiative - Complete

As a company, Pattr believes in providing our users with the highest possible security, which is why our team is hard at work building upon and improving Pattr's security, including efforts such as message encryption and password protection.

As part of this process, we have decided to upgrade our security, by purchasing an SSL Certificate. SSL is a technology that allows data traveling between its users and the server to be transmitted securely and encrypted. This ensures that no 3rd parties can "sniff" your data and encroach upon your privacy.

While we don't store messages on our servers, we do pass the messages through, scanning for certain commands such as '/nick', or '/users'. This does not store any messages on our servers, and we intend to keep it that way in effort to promote the highest security and enjoyable user experience and trust.

We asked you to help donate money as part of a campaign to raise money for this certificate, and you answered. We reached our goal, and have money left over to help pay for upgrades and some great new features to come. Thank you to all who donated and help make Pattr great.

Thank you.
